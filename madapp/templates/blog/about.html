ABOUT MAD MEANDERINGS
We are Jake and Korin Nabozny. We are filling these pages with our short stories, tall tales and endless, epic adventures.
In 2014, in the midst of planning our wedding, we made a “no-matter- what” decision to uproot our lives to Seattle, Washington. We were blessed with a few vacations to Europe and internships in Colorado during our time in college – enough time to spark our love for adventure. After college we settled in the Detroit area for a while. While Michigan is a beautiful state, it didn’t have the big mountains, trails, ocean and lakes we were eager to get lost in. Sooo… when Korin was offered a job at a big Architecture firm in downtown Seattle, we went for it!... sort of.
See, here’s the thing with us – nothing ever works out smoothly, or in ANY sort of organized fashion, but it always work out for the better! As goes one of our favorite Irish mantras, “fu** it, it’ll be grand!” Within two weeks of the big job offer and only six months before our big day, we were moving Korin across the country to settle a life in Seattle. Meanwhile, Jake (still in Detroit) hunted for jobs that would bring him back to his bride.
Just weeks before our wedding, Jake was offered an awesome techy job in Pioneer Square, Seattle. Phew! By then we’d spent 6 months apart aside from the occassional visit.
Our big day came and it was amazing chaos! Korin didn’t sleep the night before the wedding. The groomsmen were tasked with ALL of the decorating (including mowing the venue lawn). Our sweet pastor nearly forgot the part with the rings. All this to stay it was perfect for us; beautiful, ridiculous, messy madness – the best day of our lives.
